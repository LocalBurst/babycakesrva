Our Story

The heart behind
every batch

For over two decades, friends, family, and coworkers have been asking the same question: "When are you going to start selling these?"

What started as a labor of love — baking cupcakes for school events, birthday celebrations, and anyone who walked through the door — has grown into something bigger. After years of perfecting recipes and sharing her gift with thousands of happy taste-testers, Jen is finally making it official.

Every cupcake is still made the same way it always has been: from scratch, in small batches, with real ingredients and the kind of care that only comes from someone who genuinely loves what they do. The only difference now is you don't have to know her personally to get a taste.

Babycakes is home baking at its best — made in Chesterfield, Virginia, with the same love and joy that started it all.
